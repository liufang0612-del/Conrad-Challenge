Importance of Seafloor Sampling

Seabed sampling plays a crucial role in environmental monitoring and climate change assessment. Marine sediments reveal marine organism distribution, ocean floor movements, ocean circulation patterns, climate change, and global extinction events.

Key Innovations

Our design enables stable operation and high-fidelity data collection on soft and muddy seabeds. Key innovations include:

  • A dual-track system that moves easily on muddy surface.
  • A piston sampler system that can take complete samples.
  • A visual and sensing system that can monitor and identify the surrounding environment.

Vision

Roborpunk aims to make tangible contributions to global climate change research, marine biodiversity conservation and the achievement of sustainable development goals. Moreover, we plan to build a shared platform for international STEM education, stimulating the interest of global teenagers in marine science and engineering technology.
