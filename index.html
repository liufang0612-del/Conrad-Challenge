Importance of Seafloor Sampling

Seafloor sampling provides critical data on pollution, biodiversity and sediment health. Regular sampling enables timely conservation actions and better scientific understanding.

Key Innovations

Our design combines robust sampling hardware with low-power data logging and community-friendly deployment. Key innovations include:

  • Low-cost, modular sensors that are easy to service in the field.
  • Smart power management to enable long deployments on batteries and small solar panels.
  • Open data formats so communities and researchers can easily use and compare results.

Community Impact

We partner with local fishers, universities and NGOs to train volunteers, share data, and support community-led monitoring.

  • 50+ trained volunteers
  • 2 university collaborations
  • Public data dashboards and reports

Vision

Roborpunk aims to democratize seafloor sampling so that communities worldwide can monitor and protect their marine environments with scientific rigor.
